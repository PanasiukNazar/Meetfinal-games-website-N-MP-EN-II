Cookie Policy

1. Overview

This Cookie Policy explains how we use cookies and similar tracking technologies on our platform focused on gaming content, including reviews, strategies, and interactive media.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your web browser. They help websites remember your preferences, improve user experience, and gather usage data.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for website functionality and security.
  • Performance Cookies: Collect anonymous information on how visitors use our site to improve performance.
  • Functionality Cookies: Remember your preferences to provide a more personalized experience.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us analyze user behavior and measure the effectiveness of our content.

4. How We Use Cookies

Cookies enable us to:

  • Enhance site navigation and responsiveness
  • Analyze gameplay content popularity and user interaction
  • Customize content and features based on your preferences
  • Maintain security and detect fraudulent activities

5. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can control or disable cookies via your browser settings. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality or your experience.

6. Third-Party Cookies

We may allow third-party services to place cookies on our site to help with analytics, advertising, or other features. We do not control these cookies and recommend reviewing their policies.

7. Updates to This Cookie Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Updates will be posted on this page.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about our cookie practices, please contact us at meet_final@gmail.com.
